How the Aligner Works

Clear Aligners

Clear aligners are removable, transparent trays made from plastic that fit over your teeth to gradually straighten them. They are especially popular for people aged between 10 and 50, making them suitable for adults, teens, and kids. Many choose aligners as a more discreet alternative to traditional metal braces.

Wearing Clear Aligners

You will receive several sets of aligners as part of your treatment plan. These aligners must be worn for 20 to 22 hours each day to slowly shift your teeth into their correct positions, helping you achieve your ideal smile over time.

Custom-Fit Invisible Aligners

Invisible aligners, also called clear braces, are custom-made using a 3D scan of your teeth to ensure a perfect fit. The length of treatment can vary depending on how much your teeth need to move, but it usually takes between six months and two years.
