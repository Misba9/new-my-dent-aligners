Crowded Teeth

What Are Crowded Teeth?

Crowded teeth occur when there isn’t enough space in the jaw for teeth to align properly. As a result, teeth overlap, twist, or become misaligned, leading to an uneven smile. This condition can occur due to genetics, jaw size, or improper dental habits during childhood.

Why Should You Correct Crowded Teeth?

If left untreated, crowded teeth can cause oral health issues, including:

  1. 1️.Tooth Decay & Cavities – Misaligned teeth are harder to clean, leading to plaque buildup.
  2. 2️.Gum Disease – Inadequate cleaning increases the risk of gum infections and bad breath.
  3. 3️.Food Trapping Food particles get stuck in tight spaces, leading to decay.
  4. 4️.Difficulty Brushing & Flossing Overlapping teeth make oral hygiene challenging.
